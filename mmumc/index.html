Our History

In 1953, as a mission program at Walbrook Memorial Evangelical United Brethren Church, Milford Mill Church was established by dedicated Christians who answered a call to serve their Lord in a growing community. The E.U.B. and Methodist Churches merged in 1968, and Milford Mill United Methodist Church assumed its membership in the Baltimore Conference of the United Methodist Church.

Milford is now a multicultural and generational congregation in a suburban context with a beautiful campus consisting of a historical children's development center, a chapel, a 250 seat sanctuary and a parsonage. Worship is Spirit lead with 5 choirs and a learned pastor with a compassion for people and holistic health.

Pastor

Rev. Marlon B. Tilghman

The pastor of Milford Mill United Methodist Church in Pikesville, MD. Milford Mill UMC is a multi-cultural and multi-generational congregation with a membership of over 400 members.

Worship

Sundays at 10:00 a.m. in the Main Sanctuary

We have a traditional liturgy (order of worship) but a unique style of worship. Our services are inspiring, enthusiastic and welcoming. On any given Sunday we will have soloist, Gospel choir, praise band, contemporary music and/or our Handbell choirs. Each service is designed to appeal to your heart and give you courage for the days ahead.

Our Mission

To oversee programs that increase the knowledge of the Bible, foster Christian living within the family unit, and strengthen Christian fellowship for all ages.
